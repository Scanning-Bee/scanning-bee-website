    About Scanning Bee

    Annotate, scan, and visualize.

    Scanning Bee is a cross-platform, free and open source tool that allows you to annotate, scan, and visualize your real beehive using cutting-edge AI technologies. It is designed to be easy to use and highly customizable.

    The First in the World.

    Scanning Bee is the only tool in the world that allows you to see results from your beehive in real time. No need to empty your hive, no need to wait for results. Just open the app and see the results instantly.

    Features

    Anything you need, all in one place.

    Scanning Bee is packed with features that make monitoring your beehive a breeze. From annotating to scanning, it has everything you need to keep your beehive healthy and productive.

    Beehive

    Monitor your beehive with ease. Scanning Bee provides you a visual representation of your hive and its cells, so you can keep track of everything.

    AI Detection

    Scanning Bee uses cutting-edge AI technology to detect and analyze cells in your hive. Get accurate and reliable results every time.

    Database Storage

    Along with local saving options, Scanning Bee enables you to store all your data in a secure and reliable database as well.

    Annotate Cells

    Whether you're a biologist who needs to annotate cells or a beekeeper who wants to keep track of your hive, Scanning Bee has you covered.

    See Images

    See images taken directly from the hive at any time and inspect them as you like. Scanning Bee supports all common image formats.

    Security

    Your data is safe with Scanning Bee. We use the latest encryption technologies to ensure that your data is secure and protected and prevent unauthorized access.

    User Modes

    Log in as a beekeeper or a biologist and get a personalized experience. Scanning Bee adapts to your needs and provides you with the tools you need.

    Statistics

    Get detailed statistics about your hive and its cells. Scanning Bee provides you plots, graphs and tables to help you understand your hive better.

    Themes

    Choose from a variety of themes to customize the look and feel of Scanning Bee. Whether you prefer a light or dark theme, we have you covered.

    Tech Stack

    Built with the latest technologies.

    Scanning Bee is built with the latest technologies to provide you with a fast, reliable, and secure experience. We use cutting-edge technologies to ensure that you have the best possible experience and seamless communication between every layer of our application.
